Introducing Dream Valley - a new festival that fuses the world’s greatest dance music with immersive experiences

SATURDAY 27 MAY 2023 

Great Lines Heritage Park, Medway, Kent

1,000’S OF DREAMERS / 3 DREAM CAMPS / 5 STAGES / 100 ARTISTS /  IMMERSIVE ART  / DINING UNDER THE SKY / WELLBEING WORKSHOPS & SO MUCH MORE.

Celebrating the very best in dance music, the festival will boast a stellar line-up of acts.


With so much more on offer than just a music festival, Dream Valley’s immersive world merges art, dance and experiential with a plethora of different workshops and unique experiences for dreamers around the world.

After catching a live DJ set, dreamers can experience wellbeing workshops, outdoor supper clubs with a star-studded “Dining Under The Sky” experience and more.
