About Big Family Festival

Big Family Festival has gone from strength to strength its a festival for families ran by a family. Husband and wife team George and Angela, pride themselves on putting on an event that all the family can enjoy but their main focus remains on their original ethos and aim a Family Festival that really caters for children of all ages.
