IMPORTANT INFO

This is a green field site, please be aware of ground conditions.
Prolonged exposure to amplified sound may cause permanent hearing damage.
Strobe lighting and smoke effects may be used at the Event.
The Event is outdoors so you are strongly advised to bring appropriate footwear and clothing, including warm clothing for the night period.
There will be outlets on site selling food and drink.
Please respect the residents in the surrounding area and enter and leave as quietly as possible.
Please keep the field, site and nearby streets tidy, use litter bins provided.
Alcohol will not be permitted into the festival area from outside.
Drivers enter car parks at own risk, uneven surfaces likely, this is a green field site.
We do not accept any liability for break-ins, damage and/or theft.
