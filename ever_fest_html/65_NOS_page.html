The NOS Primavera Sound, which takes place every June in the Portuguese city of Porto, is part of the popular European festival family.

The line-up of the festival is made up of indie and alternative, hip-hop and electronic music and reflects, just like the larger Spanish brother, an unconditional dedication to musical diversity.

In terms of atmosphere and fan love, the Portuguese offshoot resembles the Spanish flagship festival, but the backdrop of the picturesque green park is unique in the heart of Europe's most undervalued city.
