The Jungle Weekender – Amsterdam

Friday 10th March – Monday 13th March 2023

In association with Mr Nice

.

UPDATE : NETHERLANDS HAS LIFTED ALL COVID RESTRICTIONS INCLUDING THE EU ENTRY BAN ON NON VACCINATED UK RESIDENTS... EVERYONE CAN COME & PARTY NOW!!!!

.

Just imagine if 4 of the biggest promoters in Jungle and Reggae music got together and put on a weekender in Amsterdam for over 1000 ravers…….. Well you don’t have to imagine any more because that is exactly what is going down!

.

Jungle Mania: Established in 1994 and still the Kings of the Jungle

AWOL: One of the world's longest running jungle brands spanning over 3 decades

Vibena Jungle: The true sound of underground Jungle music in London and beyond

Rompas Reggae Shack: World renowned Reggae promotion

All in association with Mr Nice London: UK leaders in CBD lifestyle inspired by Howard Marks.

.

Trust us, Amsterdam ain't even ready for this one.

.

The weekend looks like this….

.

Friday:

15:00-18:00 Meet and greet at Akhnaton nightclub in Amsterdam central. Pick up your wristbands and smoke/drink with some of your favourite artists as they play the tunes to welcome you to The Jungle Weekender.

21.00-05.00 Friday night opening party at club Panama with Jungle in room 1 and reggae / dub vibes in room 2

.

Saturday:

16:00-19:00 Reggae boat party with food, drink, smoke, chilled vibes and loads of bassline.

21:00-05.00 Saturday night event at Panama with Jungle in room 1 and upfront DnB in room 2 hosted by Cheeky Mondays

.

Sunday:

14:00-18.00 Jungle Weekender / Mr Nice boat party. The big boat party full of jungle vibes!

19:00-01:00 After Party @ Akhnaton

.

There are 4 coffee shops on board (more to be added) with exclusive takeovers to all TJW gang with a wristband

.
