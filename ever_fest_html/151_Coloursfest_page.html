About Coloursfest

Hardcore/Hardstyle
Hard Trance
Hard Dance

Glasgow's hard dance and trance spectacular Coloursfest returns in 2023 for a huge summer party with the Scottish raving staple coming together on 2023 date tbc.

Easily one of the most eagerly anticipated events in the calendar, Coloursfest has been combining dons of dance music with mind blowing production for nearly two decades, and this year will be every bit the same - if not better.

The long-running festival boasts a whopping five stages, which will be soundtracked by international, genre defining acts alongside the scene’s most promising talent.

Expect a plethora of the leading names in big room, trance and hard dance to take the gigantic roof off Glasgow’s amazing Braehead Arena.
