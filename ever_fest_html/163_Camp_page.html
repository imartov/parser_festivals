Camp Bestival is excited to announce a stellar sister show to the UK’s favourite family festival coming to Weston Park in 2023. Following hot on the heels of the pioneering Dorset-based jamboree in July, Camp Bestival Shropshire is a brand-new, stand-alone event bringing all the unique and essential family friendly elements of the original Camp Bestival to the heart of England.

Get ready to explore the wonderful world of Camp Bestival at a brand-new location. Following a hugely successful 14-year history of entertaining families, Camp Bestival Shropshire will bring the ultimate family festival experience to the heart of the Midlands. With lashings of family fun, little ones will be spoilt for choice as they step into a family festi-holiday like no other!
