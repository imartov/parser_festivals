We proudly present the next chapter of the Tidy Weekenders; Tidyland 2023!
 
In 2022 Tidyland Weekender reignited and redefined the 'Weekender' experience - many people hailed the event as 'the best Weekender ever'. With a summertime setting, outdoor stages, the biggest line up to have ever been hosted at a Weekender and a 3 year build up, it will go down in history as one of Tidy's biggest & best parties.
 
There was an overwhelming call to throw another 72 hours off from the world and somehow pull off the feat topping this year's event. After much deliberation and consideration, the next chapter of Tidyland is set to head to Pontins Holiday Centre, Southport for the next summertime seaside adventure!
 
Southport is synonymous with 'Weekender' events. The venues are able to offer a new dynamic to the Tidyland experience (more arenas, bigger production and easier accessibility for travellers and day passes), so this is bound to make 2023 an even bigger and even better Tidyland party.
 
The Tidyland Weekender is all about peace, love, unity and having fun with your chosen family. Set against the backdrop of banging music supplied by the world's best artists, get yourself to sunny Southport this summer for 3 days and 3 nights of Tidy magic!
