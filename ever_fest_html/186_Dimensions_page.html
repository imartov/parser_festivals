We’re delighted to reveal that Dimensions Festival 2023 will take place between the 31st August - 4th September.

Dimensions 2022 far exceeded anything we could have dreamed of… Dancers, DJ's, live performers, record labels, collectives and music fans, we all came together to celebrate our tenth year with one of our favourite parties to date!

In 2023, Dimensions Festival builds on last year’s 10th anniversary with a host of artists, labels and collectives from across the scene, a carefully curated list of champions of the scene, back to back exclusives, label parties, UKG celebrations, debut appearances and much more.
