You've waited long enough. The greatest escape of the festival season is back. On 6th July 2023, the fabled land of El Dorado will triumphantly open its golden gates once again for another weekend of magic and mayhem. So gather your brightest and best and prepare to explore, dance and love again in a place like no other. A place where memories are made and never forgotten.

Discovered by a vibrant travelling collective of party-starters, El Dorado is the ultimate expression of togetherness and beauty. More than just a music festival, El Dorado is an attitude. In this storybook world there is comradery, inclusivity, nature and a stiff dose of sensual hedonism. It’s an invigorating mix and there isn’t another vibe like it.

In 2023 you can expect all the favourites from years gone by: eclectic musical performers to carry you from day to night and back again, a stunning natural location, and an intimacy which makes you feel loved and blessed to be there. But on top of that, this time we’ve had two years of hard work to ensure we give you more - more pleasure, more dance and discovery, more colourful surprises, and more awareness in tandem with the natural world. What we’ve created is the most vibrant and sustainable El Dorado yet - and what is set to be the highlight of the summer.
